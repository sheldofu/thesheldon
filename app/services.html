My Skills and Experience


I am currently a client facing front-end developer for Hogarth Worldwide, and have over two years experience in the field. I work on-site at Betfair, producing responsive, interactive promotions with a combination of Javascript, jQuery, the responsive skeleton HTML framework, HTML5 and CSS3, whilst liasing with clients to advise the viability and best approach for new promotions from a technical standpoint. I am also the technical contact for an offshore team based in Dhaka, and provide them with training materials and advice.

I also do a large amount of more straightforward ‘PSD to HTML’ freelancing, which keeps my knowledge of HTML5 and CSS3 fresh. Whilst it’s impossible to learn everything, I keep up to date with the latest libraries and trends in web development, for example using Yeoman (Yo/Grunt/Bower) to scaffold personal projects. I make sure that I know enough to recognise situations on a project where a specific library or tool could be used for maximum benefit and would be worth investigating further.
